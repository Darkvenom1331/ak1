Pongal Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1/4 cup moong dal
  • 4 cups water
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 2 tbsp ghee
  • 10-12 cashews
  • 10-12 black peppercorns
  • 1 inch ginger, grated
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1-2 green chilies, chopped
  • A pinch of asafoetida
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Wash the rice and moong dal and soak them in water for 10-15 minutes.
  2. In a pressure cooker, add the soaked rice and dal along with 4 cups of water, turmeric powder, and salt. Cook for 3-4 whistles on medium heat.
  3. Once the pressure is released, open the cooker and mash the cooked rice and dal mixture using a wooden spoon or a masher.
  4. In a pan, heat ghee and add cashews, black peppercorns, grated ginger, cumin seeds, mustard seeds, green chilies, and asafoetida. Fry for a few seconds until the cashews turn golden brown.
  5. Add the fried mixture to the mashed rice and dal and mix well. Serve hot.
