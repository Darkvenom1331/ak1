Rava Upma Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rava/sooji/semolina
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 green chilli, finely chopped
  • 1/2 inch ginger, grated
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp urad dal
  • 1/2 tsp chana dal
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1/2 tsp salt, or to taste
  • 2 tbsp coriander leaves, finely chopped

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add mustard seeds. When they start to splutter, add cumin seeds, urad dal, chana dal, and curry leaves. Fry for a few seconds.
  2. Add chopped onions, green chilli, and grated ginger. Fry until onions turn translucent.
  3. Add water and salt to the pan and let it come to a boil.
  4. Slowly add rava while stirring continuously to avoid lumps. Keep stirring until the rava is cooked and the water is absorbed. This should take about 3-4 minutes.
  5. Add chopped coriander leaves and mix well.
  6. Remove from heat and serve hot with chutney or pickle.
