Butter Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb boneless chicken, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup tomato puree
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon ginger paste
  • 1 tablespoon garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt, or to taste
  • Chopped cilantro leaves for garnishing

Instructions

  • Marinate the chicken pieces with yogurt, garam masala powder, coriander powder, cumin powder, paprika, turmeric powder, and salt. Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
  • Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  • Place the marinated chicken pieces on a baking tray and bake for 15-20 minutes or until they are cooked through.
  • Meanwhile, heat the butter in a pan over medium heat. Add the ginger paste and garlic paste and sauté for a minute.
  • Add the tomato puree and cook until it thickens.
  • Add the cooked chicken pieces and mix well.
  • Add the heavy cream and mix well. Cook for 2-3 minutes or until the sauce thickens.
  • Garnish with chopped cilantro leaves.
