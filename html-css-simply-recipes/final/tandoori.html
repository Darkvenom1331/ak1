Tandoori Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg chicken, cleaned and cut into pieces
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons ginger paste
  • 2 tablespoons garlic paste
  • 2 tablespoons tandoori masala
  • 1 tablespoon red chilli powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • Salt, to taste

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, whisk together yogurt, ginger paste, garlic paste, tandoori masala, red chilli powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, lemon juice and salt.
  2. Add chicken to the mixture and mix well to coat it with the marinade. Cover the bowl and marinate in the refrigerator for at least 4 hours, or overnight for best results.
  3. Preheat oven to 200Â°C. Line a baking tray with foil and lightly grease it with oil.
  4. Arrange the marinated chicken pieces on the tray, leaving some space between each piece.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and tender. You can also grill the chicken on a barbecue for a more authentic taste.
  6. Serve hot with some mint chutney and lemon wedges on the side.
