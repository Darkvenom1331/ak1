Palak Paneer

Ingredients:

  • 200g paneer, cubed
  • 2 bunches of spinach (palak)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp red chilli powder
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

1. Blanch the spinach in boiling water for 2-3 minutes. Drain and let it cool. Once cool, blend it into a fine puree.

2. Heat oil or ghee in a pan and add cumin seeds. When they crackle, add onion and fry till golden brown.

3. Add ginger-garlic paste and fry till the raw smell goes away.

4. Add tomato and fry till it becomes soft and mushy.

5. Add coriander powder, turmeric powder, red chilli powder and garam masala powder. Fry for a minute.

6. Add the spinach puree and salt. Mix well and let it simmer for 5-10 minutes.

7. Add the paneer cubes and mix gently. Simmer for another 2-3 minutes.

8. Serve hot with roti, naan or rice.
